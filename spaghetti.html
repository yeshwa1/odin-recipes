Spaghetti

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe features al dente pasta tossed in a flavorful tomato sauce,
topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves. It's a simple and
satisfying dish that's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti pasta according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© diced onion and minced garlic until softened.
  3. Add ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Pour tomato sauce into the skillet and add Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the sauce and toss until well coated.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
